## 💚 Why KrishiHealth AI?

India has **140 million farmers**. Every day they face:

- 🌿 Crop diseases they can't identify
- 💸 Unfair mandi prices they don't know about
- 🏥 Health issues with no nearby doctor
- ☁️ Unpredictable weather destroying harvests
- 📰 No access to latest farming news

**KrishiHealth AI solves all of this — in their own language, on their phone.**

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌿 Crop Disease Detection
Upload a photo of your crop → AI instantly identifies the disease and suggests treatment. Share results directly on **WhatsApp** with one tap.

### 🤕 Injury & Health Check
Farmers work in tough conditions. Our health monitoring system helps detect injuries and health issues early — no doctor visit needed for basic guidance.

### 💰 Real-Time Mandi Prices
Live market prices from mandis across India. Know the right price before you sell — never get cheated again.

### 🤖 AI Farming Chat (24/7)
Ask anything about farming — pest control, fertilizers, soil health — in **your own language**. Available anytime, even at midnight.

### 🌦️ Hyper-Local Weather
Accurate weather forecasts for your exact location. Plan your planting and harvesting with confidence.

### 📰 Farming News & Guides
Latest agriculture news, government schemes, and expert farming guides — all in one place, in simple language.

### 🚜 Equipment Booking
Find and book tractors, harvesters, and other farm equipment nearby — no middlemen, no extra cost.

### 🏪 Market Intelligence
Compare crop prices across different markets. Choose where to sell for maximum profit.

### 📒 Digital Mill Book
Replace your paper records with a smart digital mill book. Track transactions, weights, and payments easily.

### 🔔 Smart Alerts
Get alerts for pest outbreaks, weather warnings, government schemes, and mandi price changes — before it's too late.
